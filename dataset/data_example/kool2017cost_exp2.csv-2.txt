You will be taking one of the spaceships W or K to one of the planets P or V.
The spaceships can fly to either planet, but one will mostly fly to planet P, and the other will mostly fly to planet V.
The planet a spaceship goes to most won't change during the game.
Planet P has aliens N and U, and planet V has aliens S and E on it.
Each alien has its own space treasure mine.
When you arrive at each planet, you will ask one of the aliens for space treasure from their mines.
The treasure an alien can give will change slowly during the game.
Before you choose a spaceship, you will be told whether there is a treasure multiplier.
If there is a treasure multiplier and you find treasure, you will receive 5 treasure pieces.
If there is no treasure multiplier and you find treasure, you will receive 1 treasure piece.
You can take a spaceship or ask an alien for space treasure by pressing the corresponding key.
Your goal is to get as much treasure as possible over the course of the next 200 days.
